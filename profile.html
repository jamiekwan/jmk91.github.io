About Me

I'm a designer, a strategist, and a storyteller. I know, I should've made that an alliteration, but let me elaborate on what it is I do, as I truly feel it crosses between all three of those roles. From a creative lens, I aim to use design to spark imagination in consideration of strategic goals and brand storytelling. Naturally, the idea of "play" has become central in my career thus far.

I design for play.   

I'm currently working as an Experience Designer at FORREC Ltd., within their Creative Studio. There, I help with developing the look and feel of architectural projects. I design physical and digital experiences that allow for people to engage with brand storytelling within entertainment spaces. In a nutshell, I design theme parks, entertainment centers, visitor attractions, and museums that allow guests to connect with stories -- whether brand, fictional, or historical.

+I design with play.

I'm a big believer in the power of play in unlocking innovation and seeing pain points within the community and the industry in a different light. For this reason, I co-lead Studio Bud: a creative initiative that engages Gen Z youth in design sprint workshops. I facilitate groups of youth to tackle challenges brands and non-profits face in telling their stories. I mean, who better to design with play than actual kids?

+I design through play.
